∞ Clioloop ∞
an open source self evolving AI agent
An open platform that evolves itself — running its own pipeline to gather, evaluate, and ship updates automatically.
The pipeline runs entirely on Clioloop agents in the background — not visible in the open source code.
⟳
Self-Evolving
Clioloop runs its own pipeline — gathering community input, evaluating it, and rolling out updates entirely on its own. Also available on demand.

Self-Improving
A built-in loop that continuously reviews, refines, and optimizes agent skills and behaviors in the background.
